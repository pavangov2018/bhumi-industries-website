Manufacturing Infrastructure

Machine-backed fabrication, sheet-metal processing, coating, and vehicle-body production under one industrial setup.

Bhumi Industries operates a practical manufacturing environment for e-rickshaw parts, utility bodies, sheet-metal fabrication, cutting, bending, welding, finishing, and job-work support.

Infrastructure Overview

A production floor built for sheet-metal parts, e-rickshaw body work, fabrication assemblies, and finishing operations.

Our infrastructure supports manufacturing sheet-metal parts, e-rickshaw BOP parts, fabrication-related parts, automobile sheet-metal parts, and e-rickshaw chassis body work. The facility is arranged to handle both recurring production and fabrication-led job work.

With press machines, welding stations, pipe and sheet processing equipment, CNC laser cutting, CNC sheet bending, CNC lathe support, powder coating facilities, and a tool room, Bhumi Industries is set up for practical fabrication output from raw material processing to finished assemblies.
