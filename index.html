Who We Are

Manufacturing trust through engineering discipline and practical innovation.

Bhumi Industries is a forward-looking electric vehicle and fabrication company focused on dependable products, strong build quality, and long-term operating value. We work across vehicle manufacturing, parts supply, and infrastructure-backed production to support the mobility needs of businesses, institutions, and operators.

Company Profile

Bhumi Industries combines mobility manufacturing with real production capability.

Our strength lies in blending electric vehicle manufacturing with modern fabrication practices, skilled workmanship, and disciplined process control. From chassis work and assembly integration to final finishing and delivery readiness, every stage is approached with precision and accountability.

We focus on building products that are durable, efficient, and practical for day-to-day use. That commitment helps us serve buyers who value dependable performance, responsive support, and credible manufacturing strength behind every order.
